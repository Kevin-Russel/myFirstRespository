PUP Vision

PUP sta maria envisions to offer the students of the municipality quality, relevant, responsive, and excellent tertiary education aimed to produce gradutes that are future leaders and expert in their chosen field of endeavor and contributes to the full development of the famil, community, society, and the nation.

PUP Mission

To provide high quality gradutes programs and globally-competetive human capital devlopment and community services accessible particularly to the students of the municipality.
